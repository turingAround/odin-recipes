Cotton Candy Burger Supreme

Prep Time: 3 minutes or 7 years (depending on cloud availability)

Cook Time: Until the sun sets twice

Total Time: Somewhere between a nap and forever

Servings: Enough for you and your imaginary friend

Essential Ingredients:

  • 3 freshly harvested clouds (cumulus preferred, but nimbus will do in a pinch)
  • 1 rainbow, diced into perfect cubes
  • 2 cups of unicorn giggles
  • 1 handful of stardust (must be collected during a full moon)
  • 4 slices of anti-gravity (store-bought is fine)
  • 1 bottle of bottled sunshine (vintage summer of '99)
  • 3 dreams from last Tuesday
  • 1 cotton candy bun, spun by retired circus spiders

Optional Toppings:

  • Mermaid scales (sustainably sourced)
  • Phoenix feather sprinkles (fireproof gloves required)
  • Moonbeam sauce (mild or extra sparkly)
  • Leprechaun gold shavings (must have written permission from the leprechaun)

Instructions:

  1. Begin by catching your clouds. Chase them with a butterfly net while riding a flying pig.
  2. Massage the clouds gently until they giggle. This releases the fluffiness.
  3. In a zero-gravity mixing bowl, combine unicorn giggles with stardust. Stir counterclockwise three times while hopping on one foot.
  4. Whisper your favorite knock-knock joke to the mixture. If it doesn't laugh, start over.
  5. Shape your cloud mixture into patties using telekinesis (or a spatula if your powers are weak today).
  6. Grill on the dark side of the moon for exactly 7 shooting stars.
  7. Toast your cotton candy bun under a phoenix's wing (medium flame).
  8. Stack ingredients in order of their magical properties, being careful not to wake the dreams.
  9. Serve immediately before it floats away.

Warning Labels:

  • Side effects may include spontaneous levitation and uncontrollable rainbow sneezing.
  • Not responsible for any temporary invisibility lasting more than 4 hours.
  • Do not consume if you're allergic to whimsy.
  • Keep away from serious adults and gravity enthusiasts.

Pro Tips:

  • If clouds are out of season, substitute with dragon's breath cotton candy.
  • Best eaten while floating upside down under a blue moon.
  • Pairs well with liquidified laughter or meteor juice.
